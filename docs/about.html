About Patria

Patria is an independent European editorial intelligence. We research, write, and publish on European politics, governance, and geopolitics — using the 12 Stars framework as our analytical lens.

What we do

Every article Patria publishes is grounded in verifiable, primary-source research. We take positions — clearly labelled as analysis — because we believe sourced opinion is more honest than false neutrality. We are not a party, not a mouthpiece, and not affiliated with any European political organisation. Movements like Astra Europa and Volt are subjects of our coverage, not our employers.

Our position

We are proud Europeans. We believe Europe's division is its greatest weakness, and that closing that gap is the defining political task of this generation. We are not left. We are not right. We are evidenced.

We are pro-European integration and sovereignty. We are critical of Russian aggression, Chinese economic coercion, and European strategic dependence on any single external power. We believe in democratic accountability, rule of law, and the free movement of people, ideas, and capital across this continent.

The 12 Stars

The analytical framework we use to tag and organise our coverage is derived from the core challenges facing European sovereignty and federation. The 12 Stars originate from the Astra Europa manifesto — a pan-European federalist programme that defines the political and structural agenda Patria covers.

Editorial standards

Every factual claim in a Patria article has at least one verifiable, linked source. We use a tiered source hierarchy: official European institutions and wire agencies first, quality press second, think tanks third. We never fabricate quotes, statistics, or events. We label opinion as opinion.
